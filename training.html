Training Schedule for Session 1 and Session 2

Session 1 Participants

Session 1 Participants are those who are 12 years old and under. This session is focused on getting the students comfortable around others and learning the basic commands, numbers, and forms in Korean.

Session 2 Participants

Session 2 participants are those who are 13 years of age and older. This session is advanced and houses majority of veteran students and some beginners. Students will study and master forms 1 through 8 (depending on their belt color) as well as perform self-defense techniques and forms with a wodden staff (or a stick).
